## What this guidebook solves

<div class="cards">
  <div class="panel">
    <strong>Inconsistent spacing</strong>
    Repeated tiny layout mismatches between pages and components.
  </div>
  <div class="panel">
    <strong>Weak hierarchy</strong>
    Headings, labels, and body text that don’t guide the eye.
  </div>
  <div class="panel">
    <strong>Random component sizing</strong>
    Buttons, fields, and cards that feel unrelated.
  </div>
  <div class="panel">
    <strong>Polish regressions</strong>
    UI quality drops during fast product iteration.
  </div>
</div>
